About Me

The more I learn, the less I know.

My design journey started with the desire of a more inclusive world. I believe that improving accessibility with better experience is one of the best approach. The purpose as a designer for me is making things more convenient to the majority, and seeking ways to be a bridge between needs and productions. My current role is building user-friendly products that not only increase working efficiency and effectiveness for the pros, but smooth the learning curve for novice.

Out of work, I’m passionate about traveling, civic activities, skateboarding and TRX. There are 3 criteria I set in my career path: For the greater good, challenges, and growing market.
